Welcome to Singleton Theory

In mathematics the Singleton is known as a set with exactly one element. This is symbolized with the markdown of: {0}. As far as the ideas of our product suite goes, it may not contain one element, but it is built as a single element giving you access to a whole range of products, development frameworks and ideas. All this is made for one simple thing: To add value to our clients while adding value to the open source community who we see as our colleagues, peers and in some cases even our clients.

While this is very fuzzy at this stage, stay tuned as we make complex things simple.
